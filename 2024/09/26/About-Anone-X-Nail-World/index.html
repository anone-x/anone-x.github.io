Welcome to Anone X Nail World!

Hello and welcome to my blog about all things nail-related! Here, you’ll find the latest nail art designs, manicure techniques, nail tech tips, nail tutorials, and general nail care knowledge.

About Me

I’m Anone, and I’ve been working in the gel nail industry since 2020. For the past four years, I’ve been with a company that manufactures and wholesales UV nail gel for private labels. As a copywriter introducing new products, I’ve become very familiar with gel nails.

Why This Blog?

I’ve always wanted to share my knowledge and passion for gel nails. This blog is my way of expressing what I’ve learned over the years. It’s a journey that started with me asking myself, “What am I good at?” The answer led me here, creating content about gel nails.

My Journey

Building this blog has been a learning experience. I’ve taught myself website design and coding from scratch. It’s been challenging, but I’m committed to continuing this journey and improving along the way.

Support

If you enjoy my content, please consider bookmarking this site and sharing it with others. Your support means a lot!

Let’s Learn Together

I’m excited to dive into the world of gel nails with you! By the way, my avatar features a subtitle in Japanese that says “Dress up for better inspiration!” Feel free to call me Anone – I love this name!

Let’s embark on this nail adventure together!
